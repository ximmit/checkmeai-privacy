Privacy Policy for CheckMeAi

Effective date: September 7, 2025

Who we are Data we collect How we use data Third-party services Retention & deletion Your rights International transfers Security Children Changes Contact

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share data with advertisers and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

1) Who we are (Data Controller)

CheckMeAi (“we”, “us”, “our”). Contact: privacy@checkmeai.space

2) Information we collect

  • Contact info: Email (for account login, support, and password reset)
  • Identifiers: Internal user ID; Apple identifiers necessary for purchases
  • Purchases: Subscription status, product IDs, transaction receipts (payment processing is handled by Apple; we do not receive your card details)
  • User content: PDFs you upload, prompts, study materials
  • Audio data: Voice input you choose to record for quizzes (processed into text)
  • Usage & diagnostics: Quiz attempts, progress, scores, app crash logs, and performance metrics

We do not collect precise location data.

3) How we use information (Purposes & legal bases)

  • Provide the app and features (authentication, quiz generation, syncing, subscriptions) — performance of a contract.
  • Improve stability, security, and support (including diagnostics) — legitimate interests.
  • Comply with legal, tax, and fraud-prevention obligations — legal obligation.

Where consent is required (for example, microphone access), we request it in the app.

4) What we do not do

  • Sell personal data
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Track users across apps or websites

5) Third-party services (Processors)

We share data only with service providers who help us operate the app. They process data on our behalf under contracts:

  • Apple In-App Purchases — process purchases and receipts
  • RevenueCat — manage subscriptions and entitlements
  • Crash/analytics (e.g., Apple, Crashlytics/Sentry) — diagnostics and performance
  • Cloud hosting/storage (e.g., AWS/GCP) — backend services and secure storage
  • Speech-to-text (e.g., on-device Apple Speech or a provider processing your audio solely for transcription)

We do not allow these providers to use your data for their own advertising purposes.

6) Data retention & deletion

  • Account data is kept while your account is active.
  • Purchase/receipt data may be retained for up to 7 years for legal/tax reasons.
  • Diagnostics are typically retained up to 12 months.
  • Audio uploads used only for transcription are deleted after processing unless you save them as content.

You can request deletion at any time by emailing privacy@checkmeai.space. If you delete your account, we erase or anonymize personal data unless we must retain certain records by law.

7) Your rights

Depending on your region (e.g., EU/EEA, UK, California), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, email privacy@checkmeai.space. We will respond within applicable legal timeframes (typically 30 days).

Additional regional notes
  • EEA/UK: You may lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.
  • California: We do not “sell” personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA.

8) International data transfers

If we transfer your data outside your region (for example, from the EEA), we use lawful safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms.

9) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10) Children’s privacy

CheckMeAi is not intended for children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal data from these age groups. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new version with an updated “Effective date,” and we may notify you in-app or by email for material changes.

12) Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@checkmeai.space
