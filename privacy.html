Privacy Policy for CheckMeAi

Effective date: September 7, 2025 • App Store content rating: 4+

Who we are Data we collect How we use data What we don’t do Third-party services Retention & deletion Your rights Security Children Changes Contact & EULA App Privacy summary

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share data with advertisers and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

1) Who we are

CheckMeAi (“we”, “us”, “our”). Contact: privacy@checkmeai.space

2) Information we collect

  • Contact info: Email address (for account login, support, and password reset)
  • Identifiers: Internal user ID (used to operate your account)
  • Purchases: Subscription status, product identifiers, and Apple transaction receipts (payment processing is handled by Apple — we do not receive your card details)
  • User content: Materials you choose to upload or create in the app (e.g., PDFs, prompts, study notes)

We do not collect audio recordings, usage analytics, crash/diagnostic data, precise location, or advertising identifiers.

3) How we use information

  • Provide the app and its features (authentication, content syncing, and subscriptions).
  • Support and account communications (respond to requests, account notices).
  • Comply with legal and fraud-prevention obligations (e.g., purchase records required by law).

We do not use your data for targeted advertising or cross-app tracking.

4) What we do not do

  • Sell personal data
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Track users across other apps or websites
  • Collect audio recordings, usage analytics, or crash/diagnostic data

5) Third-party services

We rely only on the following service provider to operate in-app purchases:

  • Apple In-App Purchases — processes subscriptions and in-app purchases and provides transaction receipts.

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

6) Data retention & deletion

  • Account data (email, user ID) is retained while your account is active.
  • Purchase/receipt records may be retained for up to 7 years to satisfy legal and tax requirements.
  • User content remains until you delete it or delete your account.

You may request deletion at any time by emailing privacy@checkmeai.space. If you delete your account, we erase or anonymize personal data unless we must keep certain records to comply with the law.

7) Your rights

Depending on your region (e.g., EU/EEA, UK, California), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@checkmeai.space. We will respond within applicable legal timeframes (typically 30 days).

8) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9) Children’s privacy

Content rating: 4+ on the App Store. CheckMeAi is designed for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are under 13, please use the app with a parent or guardian and do not submit personal information (such as an email address). Parents or guardians may contact us to request removal of any information.

10) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post any changes here with an updated “Effective date,” and may provide in-app notice for material updates.

11) Contact & Terms of Use (EULA)

Contact email: privacy@checkmeai.space

  • Tracking: None (no cross-app tracking; no targeted advertising).
